Ray Singer

I design and make all sorts of stuff

Hi I'm Ray and I love to design and make things. This passion has lead me on journeys from clothing and graphic design, into web design, web development and software engineering. I also have a huge interest in sound design and audio production.

My current field of interest and obsession is UX Design. I'm busy learning everything I can about this sub-genre of design. I'm leaning on my previous experience, while combining it with a wealth of new info.

Design can be a solo pursuit or a team activity. I enjoy both, but especially those challenging projects tackled with an enthusiastic team.

When I'm not doing the above mentioned things you won't find me watching TV, ever! You might catch me out in the surf testing some fins I have made, on the estuary kiteboarding or stand-up paddle boarding with my family.
